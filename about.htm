About Us

MamaPutOnline was birthed out of a clear vision: making authentic, smoky local delicacies accessible to corporate professionals without losing that traditional home-cooked essence.

The Inception Challenges

Launching a food logistics framework in Port Harcourt came with severe logistical bottlenecks. We faced heavy distribution challenges, preserving food temperature during rush hour gridlocks, and building digital trust with customers who were skeptical of buying local soups online.

The Road to Success

By optimizing our heat-insulated courier delivery routes and committing to an exact 5-minute response matrix via WhatsApp, we converted initial hurdles into our greatest edge. Today, we confidently serve premium corporate hubs across the district.
